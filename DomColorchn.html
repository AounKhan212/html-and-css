Lahore is the capital city of Punjab province. It is the second-largest city of Pakistan after Karachi. The city is often called the "Heart of Pakistan." Lahore has a population of over 13 million people. It is one of the most culturally vibrant cities in South Asia. Lahore has a history that spans more than a thousand years.
