.Lahore is the capital city of Punjab province. It is the second-largest city of Pakistan after Karachi. The city is often called the "Heart of Pakistan." Lahore has a population of over 13 million people. It is one of the most culturally vibrant cities in South Asia. Lahore has a history that spans more than a thousand years. The city has been ruled by Ghaznavids, Mughals, Sikhs, and the British. Mughal emperors made Lahore an important center of art and architecture. The famous Lahore Fort and Shalimar Gardens are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. The Badshahi Mosque is one of the largest mosques in the world. Lahore is home to the historic Walled City with its 13 gates. Delhi Gate, Bhati Gate, and Lohari Gate are among the most famous.
